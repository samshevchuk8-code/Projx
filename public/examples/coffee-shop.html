Our Story

Started by two neighbors who just wanted a better cup.

In 2018 we took over a tiny storefront with a secondhand espresso machine and a stubborn belief that a café should feel like a friend's kitchen. We still roast every bean ourselves.

Pull up a worn wooden chair, meet the regulars, and stay a while.
